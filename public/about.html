MultiplayerSet

  • MultiplayerSet currently makes use of the following open source projects:

    • Express, by TJ Holowaychuk
    • jQuery, by John Resig
    • nodeJS, by Ryan Dahl
    • Socket.IO, by Guillermo Rauch

    MultiplayerSet is a realtime multiplayer game written in nodeJS on the server and jQuery on the client.

    • Vincent Woo is a programmer working for Google. He did all the computery stuff.
    • Tiffany Enriquez is a designer who works at eBay. She did the graphics and layouting.

    For all inquiries, please email Vincent.
